On Fire

Essence of Project

The video was submitted to the client as a presentation
ticket for the Roger Hautchel Student Academy. The brief
for the project was: “explain in a 2 minutes video why you
are a creative and why we should pick you as a candidate to
our festival”. The work tries to embody and show what does it look like to
live as a creative. Trigger the audience using the allegory
of fuel and fire to represent ideas and creativity,
everithing packed up in a 120” short film totally written
and directed by me.
